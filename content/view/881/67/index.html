Volunteers Needed August 1-15, 2006
Participate
Written by Michela Pilo   
Jul 06, 2006 at 02:33 PM

Technical Support Team/Artist Management Team/Public Information Team

Festival Overview: ZeroOne San Jose: A Global Festival of Art on the Edge will showcase the world's most innovative contemporary artists at the intersection of art and technology. ZeroOne San Jose is a multi-dimensional, startling and brilliant audience event with exhibits, live cinema, performances, workshops and youth activities. ZeroOne is about artists making art using technology as a tool, but not about technology for its own sake. Concurrently, the 13th International Symposium on Electronic Art (ISEA2006) will be held August 7-13, 2006, in San Jose, California. The 2006 edition of the internationally renowned ISEA Symposium focuses on the critical, theoretical and pragmatic exploration of four important themes: Transvergence, Interactive City, Community Domain and Pacific Rim.

Volunteer Overview: The Volunteer program offers individuals the opportunity to become an integral part of the ISEA Symposium and the ZeroOne San Jose Festival.

This experience presents opportunities to meet and learn directly from interactive artists and industry professionals and network with those who have similar interests. Festival Volunteers assist festival attendees and support festival programs and events. Volunteers will also have free admission to all programs, the symposium, selected performances and many special events. Festival dates: August 7th–13th, 2006. For more information, log on to www.01sj.org We are looking for reliable, hard-working, self-starting, independent, enthusiastic individuals who are excited about the ZeroOne San Jose/ISEA2006 Festival and want to be part of a Global Festival of Art on the Edge.

Areas of interest:

Technical Support Team: Connectivity and technology infrastructure, including installation and break-down of interactive artworks and troubleshooting during the event

Artist Management Team: Artist & symposium participant registration, artist relations including assistance with navigating San Jose, residence issues, material and installation/break-down support

Public Information Team: General registration, docents and guides, administrative support at Festival headquarters, education programs and event staffing

Qualifications: Interest and experience in art & technology, production, artist relations, marketing, education or festival administration. Undergraduate and graduate students welcome.

 

  • Must be organized, enthusiastic, and dedicated to helping the Festival run smoothly for everyone.
  • Must be able to take initiative and solve problems in this dynamic and self-motivated role.
  • Must be effective in dealing with groups of people and performing under pressure.
  • All volunteers are responsible for their own travel to and from downtown San Jose, as well as their own meals. Budget accommodations can be arranged through the Festival.
  • Must be able to attend an orientation (days and dates to be confirmed)

 

Availability: Approximately 20 hours of volunteer time from 1st August – 15th August in return for a free festival and symposium pass. Or volunteer 8 hours or more for a free festival pass.

Submit your letter of interest -dowload pdf- application and resume via e-mail to: Volunteer Coordinator, ZeroOne San Jose.

ZeroOne San Jose Festival has a strong commitment to diversity.
