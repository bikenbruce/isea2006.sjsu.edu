PigeonBlog release – 8:00-8:15pm daily from the Fairmont Plaza

Digital Kajuku (illumination of City Hall Rotunda) by Akira Hasegawa 9:30pm – 2:00am

C4F3 – Have lunch at this interactive café at the San Jose Museum of Art. Real food is served and the walls react to sound. Café hours are 10:00am – 10:00pm Mon-Sat; 12-5pm Sun.

Container Culture Works Gallery Container Culture is an exhibition of art works that travel from different port cities that rim the Pacific in standardized containers. ZeroOne San Jose brings CONTAINER CULTURE to San Jose to be presented alongside each other; almost like a conference of containers. CONTAINER CULTURE is a site-specific, international art gallery featuring artworks from Auckland, Beijing, Hong Kong, Oakland/San Jose, Mumbai, Singapore, Seoul, Tokyo, and Vancouver.
