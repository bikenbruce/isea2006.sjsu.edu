On-Net Surveillance Systems, Inc. (OnSSI)
Investor
Written by Michela Pilo   
Jul 25, 2006 at 08:45 AM

OnSSI is a NY-based market leader in non-proprietary, open-architecture IP Video Delivery solutions. OnSSI’s Camera Management/Recording platforms and Virtual Matrix Switches are geared toward intelligent event detection, convergence with physical security systems, and comprehensive video distribution to users via multiple clients. OnSSI currently powers over 7,500 IP Video Delivery systems.
