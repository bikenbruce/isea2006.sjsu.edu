Official Airline ZeroOne San Jose and ISEA2006 Symposium
American Airlines
Written by Steve Dietz   
Apr 27, 2006 at 07:31 PM

Official Carrier

Do You Know the Way to San Jose? Norman Y. Mineta San Jose International Airport is the Gateway Airport to ZeroOne San Jose Festival and ISEA2006 Symposium see them on the web at: http://www.sjc.org/

We are pleased to announce American Airlines as an official carrier for ZeroOne San Jose/ISEA2006. American Airlines is offering Festival attendees a 5% discount on full fares for travel to San Jose, San Francisco or Oakland. Please call 1-800-433-1790 and refer to STARfile number A2656BH.

American Airlines has arranged for the attendees to the Festival/Symposium the following discount:

5% discount off Coach and First Class fares. After the ticket is purchased, but prior to outbound flight departure date, the name may be changed for a service fee.

The Coach Class discount applies to most applicable fares that are available for your date and time of travel. However, fares that are already discounted will not be eligible for the 5% discount.

For fare and schedule information and to make your reservations, please call the American Airlines Meeting Services at 1-800-433-1790 and provide the discount code A2656BH. Their hours of operation are 5 AM to 12 midnight Central Time every day of the year.

This discount is valid the 1st of May thru the 27th of August 2006, for travel to San Jose, San Francisco or Oakland, California.

You may review fare and schedule information and make reservations at American Airlines web site: www.AA.COM
